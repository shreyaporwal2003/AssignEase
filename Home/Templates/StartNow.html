How to Use AssignEase

Step 1: Login or Sign Up

Click on the Login button if you already have an account. Otherwise, click Sign Up to create a new account. Choose your role as Student or Teacher during registration.

Step 2: Navigate the Dashboard

Once logged in, you will be redirected to the dashboard. Use the menu to explore different sections, such as:

  • View assignments
  • Submit assignments
  • Check deadlines
  • Track your progress

Step 3: Submitting an Assignment

To submit an assignment:

  1. Select the course from the dashboard.
  2. Click on Submit Assignment.
  3. Upload your file (accepted formats: PDF, DOCX).
  4. Click Submit.

Step 4: Viewing Grades

Grades for submitted assignments can be viewed under the Grades section. Navigate to the course and select the Grades tab.

FAQs

For common queries, visit the Help section. You can also contact your instructor through the Contact tab in the dashboard.
